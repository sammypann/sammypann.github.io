The Problem

The lifestyle of a college student can be extremely detrimental to their health. Between academic pressure, social pressure, future career planning pressure, etc., university students oftentimes find themselves partaking in unhealthy habits that will affect them later on in life. These stressors can lead to binge drinking, lack of sleep, a poor diet, and even more that will end in illness or the inability to perform at their best. For our project, we wanted to showcase how to lead a healthier lifestyle and display how to improve the health of the college experience.

The Data
